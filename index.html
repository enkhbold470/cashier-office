💼 De Anza College – Spring 2025

📚 Tuition

  • $31/unit (CA residents)
  • $245/unit (F-1 Intl.)
  • $58.75 campus fees/quarter
  • $552 health insurance (F-1)

🔒 F-1 Student Policy

  • Classes are not automatically dropped
  • Full payment due by Apr 18
  • Account hold after Week 6
  • Use MyPortal to enroll/pay

💳 Payment Options

  • Online: Visa / MasterCard
  • Flywire (Intl. transfers)
  • Check (mail or in-person)
  • Cash (exact change)

📆 Installment Plan

  • Enroll by Apr 18 in MyPortal
  • $25 fee
  • 3 payments auto-charged
  • Adjusts with class changes

💸 Refund Info

  • Drop by 2nd Sun of quarter
  • Request in MyPortal > Refund
  • Refund = credit next term
  • $10 fee after term starts

🖨️ Invoices

  • Log into MyPortal → Ebill
  • Select the correct quarter
  • Click “View Statement”
  • Print/save PDF for records
