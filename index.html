De Anza College | Spring 2025 Payment Information

Tuition & Fees

  • $31/unit – Enrollment Fee
  • $245/unit – Foreign Tuition
  • $58.75 – Campus Fees (includes Eco Pass, Health Services)
  • $552 – Mandatory Health Insurance (for F-1 students)

F-1 Visa Student Payment Policy

  • No drop for non-payment
  • Full payment due by April 18
  • Account holds placed after Week 6 for unpaid balances
  • Installment plan available via MyPortal

Payment Methods

  • Online payment: Visa, MasterCard, Discover
  • Flywire (processing delay: 2 days)
  • Mail-in checks or in-person delivery
  • Cash payments accepted (exact amount recommended)

Installment Payment Plan

  • Enroll via MyPortal by April 18
  • One-time $25 setup fee
  • Three automatic credit card charges
  • Adjusts automatically with unit changes

Refund Policy

  • Refund deadline: 2nd Sunday of the quarter
  • Drop by deadline to qualify for a refund
  • Request via MyPortal > Bill Payment > Refund Request
  • $10 fee if dropped after term begins
  • Credits carry over to future quarters

Printing Invoices

  • Login to MyPortal > Ebill app
  • Select the academic term
  • Click "View Statement" and print
